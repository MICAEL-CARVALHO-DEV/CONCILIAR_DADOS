# Prompts - Fundo de Login SaaS (SEC Bahia)

Use estes prompts para gerar variações de fundo no mesmo estilo institucional do login.

```text
modern tropical campus building with minimalist concrete and glass architecture, wide central walkway leading to the entrance, palm trees on both sides, warm sunset sky with soft orange light, landscaped green garden and reflecting water feature, cinematic composition, shallow depth of field, slightly blurred background for login UI, professional institutional atmosphere, ultra realistic, 16:9, 1920x1080
```

clean modern university campus with palm trees and minimal landscaping, large glass building and concrete structure, wide pathway leading to entrance, tropical environment at golden hour sunset, soft warm lighting, shallow depth of field blur to support UI overlay, minimal visual noise, ideal background for SaaS login page, ultra realistic, 16:9, 1920x1080

## Prompt 3 (institucional brasileiro)

```text
modern Brazilian public school architecture with palm trees, minimalist building design, large windows and clean concrete structure, tropical landscape, sunset golden hour lighting, cinematic depth of field, slightly blurred for website login background, professional institutional look, realistic render, 16:9
```

## Sufixo recomendado para qualquer prompt

```text
soft background blur, shallow depth of field, low visual noise, ideal for website login background
```

## Configuração recomendada da IA

- Aspect ratio: `16:9`
- Resolução: `1920x1080` (ou 4K)
- Estilo: `realistic`
- Iluminação: `golden hour`

## Como concluir no projeto

1. Escolha a variação com melhor legibilidade.
2. Salve como `assets/login-bg.jpg`.
3. Salve o brasão em PNG transparente como `assets/sec-logo.png`.
4. Faça `Ctrl+F5` no navegador.

## Observação prática

Se o fundo ficar poluído, o formulário perde leitura. Prefira cenários limpos, com foco em caminho central, prédio e palmeiras.
